    About Wyvern

    Wyvern is a deep, command-oriented MMORPG with hundreds of commands for social actions, crafting, inventory management, and more. Players can create custom aliases, macros, and hotkeys for flexible gameplay. With a free-to-play model, nearly all content is accessible to everyone.

    Features

    • 17 unique player races: humans, elves, fighting cats, giants, vampires, and more
    • Over 50 quests with unique rewards
    • Builder Mode for custom areas and adventures
    • Guild system to specialize abilities
    • 100+ unique spells and magical items
    • Hundreds of playable areas: towns, dungeons, ruins, underwater realms
    • 500+ monsters with weapons, spells, and summoning abilities
    • Random Dungeon system for endless exploration
    • Raid bosses, live quests, and other endgame events

    Gameplay & Mechanics

    • 2D tile-based game with dynamic lighting and day/night cycles
    • Missiles, projectiles, and area-of-effect spells
    • Interactive objects: pushable gates, traps, vehicles
    • Multiple damage types, resistances, and vulnerabilities
    • Side-scroller engine for certain quests and areas
    • Advanced camera system for detection and scrying
    • Realistic body-part system for layered equipment
    • Economy, quests, and guild systems supported by thousands of objects

    Cross-Platform Support

    Wyvern runs on Windows, Mac, Linux (non-Steam), iOS, and Android. All platforms share the same servers for seamless multiplayer. Accounts can be linked via Steam, Gmail, Apple, or Facebook.

    Learning Curve

    Wyvern has a steep learning curve. Tutorials exist, but mastery rewards players with an immersive MMORPG experience.
